Quick Video Editing

cutvid.io is a video editor that run in modern browsers.
With cutvid.io, you can:

  • Combine & cut videos.
  • Add audio tracks, images, texts, visual effects.
  • Save projects and share videos.

Try it!

About

cutvid.io is aimed at people who are not video profesionnals but still want to create and share videos.

cutvid.io is open source and built to grow with an extensible design.

How it works

Place a sequence, upload a video, move it, cut it, add effects.

Then render & share your video!

Questions you might have

What is a render?

Rendering is the last step when creating a video. When rendering, the video is built so you can eventually download it.
This process takes some time.



What is a LQ render?

LQ renders happen directly in your browser.
Since current browser video technology is quite limited, this does not produce the best quality available.
However, it could be good enough depending on what you are doing.
If you are making a dank meme for your friend, go for LQ.



What is an HQ render?

In HQ renders, all frames of videos are extracted, then combined as smoothly as possible on cutvid.io's server.
When this process is done, you receive an email with a link to download your video.
If you are presenting to some important group/person, you should buy an HQ render.
If you are publishing a video advertisement, you should buy an HQ render.



How do I contact the owner?

What is your tech stack?

Frontend: Vue.js, GLSL for effects.
Some plugins use third party libraries (canvas plugin uses fabric.js)
Backend: Python, Rails, ffmpeg for HQ videos



Any business plan/features?

Not yet, but these could include enough HQ renders for your employees, project storage, private instance of the app, generation of big quantities of personalized videos, removed time limits for shared videos and many other features.
