About

For some background info:

This app, cutvid.io (previously lattefx.com) is an online video editing platform. I started it in 2019 after creating special effects and trying to sell these online, following my brother's advice (hence the name lattefx - special effects at coffee shop prices). I worked on it many nights of my last university year. After some research, I found out that almost nobody wanted to buy special effects, but that a lot of people needed to cut/trim/edit videos in a web browser. That's why I turned lattefx into a more general purpose video editor, then renamed it to cutvid.io.

After renaming to cutvid.io, I focussed on making the product useful to more people by writing documentation, fixing bugs and trying to make the app more user-friendly every day.

Why use cutvid.io

Depending on who you are, you might want to:

  • Cut some parts of a screengrab to show your friends or colleages;
  • Create ads to show the products you sell;
  • Edit an a video for a school project;
  • Show of the work you do in a video portfolio;
  • Add music to a video;
  • Add pictures to a video;
  • Add music, pictures, title, and text over a video;
  • Rotate a video you grabbed with your phone and tweak the colors.
You should be able to do all of this here.
