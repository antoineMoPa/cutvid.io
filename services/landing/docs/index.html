About cutvid.io

This app, cutvid.io (previously lattefx.com) is an online video editing platform. I started it after creating special effects and trying to sell these online, following my brother's advice. After some research, I found out that the demand was not high for special effects, but that a lot of people needed to cut/trim/edit videos in a web browser. That's how lattefx was born, then renamed to cutvid.io.
